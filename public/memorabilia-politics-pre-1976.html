Although CNN didn’t launch until June 1980, I’ve tried to collect material from earlier presidential cycles. Hopefully, these items will add perspective to the post-1976 material. The items were obtained from numerous sources, including other collectors, auction sites, antique shops and online sites. With so many years to choose from, this section should grow significantly in the future.

Row 1: Calvin Coolidge in 1925; Theodore Roosevelt in 1885; Warren G. Harding at the White House; Row 2: Joseph Stalin, Franklin D. Roosevelt and Winston Churchill; John F. Kennedy being interviewed by Walter Cronkite; William Howard Taft in Manassas.
